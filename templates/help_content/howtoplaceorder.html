How to Place an Order

Follow these simple steps to place an order.

Ordering Steps

Step 1: Log in to Your Account

  • Go to Onlinetech.com and log in to your account.
  • If you don’t have an account, sign up with your email and create a password.

Step 2: Browse and Select Items

  • Use the search bar or categories to find the items you want.
  • Click on a product to view details, price, and availability.
  • Click "Add to Cart" to add the item to your shopping cart.

Step 3: Proceed to Checkout

  • Click on the cart icon and review your selected items.
  • Click "Proceed to Checkout" when you’re ready to order.

Step 4: Enter Shipping Details

  • Provide your delivery address and contact details.
  • Choose your preferred delivery method.

Step 5: Choose Payment Method

  • Select your preferred payment option:
    • Credit/Debit Card
    • Mobile Money
    • Cash on Delivery (if available)
    • Voucher Code

Step 6: Confirm and Place Order

  • Review your order summary and total cost.
  • Click "Place Order" to complete your purchase.
  • You will receive a confirmation email with order details.
