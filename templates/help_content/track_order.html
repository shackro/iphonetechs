How to Track Your Order

Tracking your order on is easy and ensures you stay updated on your delivery status.

Tracking Steps

Step 1: Log in to Your Account

  • Go to Onlinetech.com and log in to your account on menu dropdown list.
  • Enter your email and password to access your account.

Step 2: Go to "My Orders"

  • Once logged in, click on your menu icon and select "Profile" from the dropdown menu.
  • You will see a list of all your recent purchases.

Step 3: Select Your Order

  • Click on the specific order you want to track.
  • You'll be redirected to the order details page.

Step 4: Track Your Order

  • On the order details page, you'll see the current status of your order, such as "processing" , "Sorting for shipping," or "Out for Delivery."
  • If the order has been shipped, you can find a tracking number and carrier details to track your package in real-time.

Step 5: Receive Notifications

  • We sends email and SMS updates on your order status.
  • Enable notifications in your account settings to stay informed about your delivery.

If you experience any issues tracking your order, contact Our customer support at 0711 011 011 for assistance.
