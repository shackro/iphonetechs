How to Cancel Your Order

If you've placed an order on Jumia and need to cancel it, follow these simple steps.

Cancellation Steps

Step 1: Log in to Your Jumia Account

  • Go to Jumia.com and log in to your account.
  • Enter your email and password to access your order details.

Step 2: Navigate to "My Orders"

  • Click on your profile icon and select "My Orders" from the dropdown menu.
  • Find the order you wish to cancel.

Step 3: Select the Order

  • Click on the specific order you want to cancel.
  • Check the order status to ensure it is still eligible for cancellation.

Step 4: Click "Cancel Order"

  • If your order is eligible for cancellation, you will see a "Cancel Order" button.
  • Click on it and follow the prompts to confirm the cancellation.

Step 5: Receive Confirmation

  • Once the cancellation is successful, you will receive an email confirmation.
  • If you paid online, the refund process will be initiated automatically.

If you experience any issues canceling your order, contact Our customer support at 0711 011 011 for assistance.
