How to Request a Refund or Return

If you are not satisfied with your order, you can request a refund or return following these steps.

Return & Refund Steps

Step 1: Check Return Eligibility

  • Ensure the item is eligible for return. Check Our return policy.
  • The item should be in its original condition with packaging intact.

Step 2: Log in to Your Jumia Account

  • Go to Onlinetech.com and log in to your account.
  • Enter your email and password to access your orders.

Step 3: Navigate to "My Orders"

  • Click on your profile icon and select "My Orders."
  • Find the order containing the item you wish to return.

Step 4: Request a Return

  • Select the item and click "Return Item."
  • Choose the reason for the return and submit your request.

Step 5: Return the Item

  • Follow the return instructions provided.
  • Drop off the item at the designated return center or arrange for pickup if applicable.

Step 6: Receive Your Refund

  • Once the item is received and verified, We will process your refund.
  • Refunds are issued via the original payment method or as store credit.
