How to Pay for Your Order

We offers multiple payment options. Pay for the item on platform.

Payment Options

Option 1: Pay with Credit Card

Using a credit card for your online purchases offers convenience and security, with features like zero-liability fraud protection and the potential to earn rewards on every transaction. With saved payment details, you can shop effortlessly and enjoy financial flexibility through interest-free periods or installment plans. Experience the ease of shopping while maximizing your savings and benefits with credit card payments.

Option 2: PayPal

PayPal enhances your online shopping experience by providing an extra layer of security, as your financial information is never shared with merchants. It allows for quick transactions without repeatedly entering card details and offers buyer protection on eligible purchases for peace of mind. Enjoy a seamless and stress-free shopping journey with PayPal, widely accepted across numerous online retailers.

Option 3: Vouchers

You can pay for your orders using a voucher code which is applied prior to checkout.

Choose the one that works best for you for a hassle-free shopping experience.

Paying for Your Order - A Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Place Your Order

  • Browse the website and select the items you'd like to purchase.
  • Add the items to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  • Review your order details and make any necessary changes.

Step 2: Choose Your Payment Method

  • Select your preferred payment method from the options available:
    • Online Payment: Pay using your credit/debit card or online banking.
    • Online Payment: Choose to pay with PayPal
    • Voucher Code: Redeem a voucher code for payment prior to checkout.

Step 3: Complete Your Payment

  • If you've chosen online payment, you'll be redirected to a secure payment page where you can enter your payment details.
  • If you've chosen online payment for Paypal, you'll be redirected to Your paypal website for login
  • To pay with a voucher code:
    • Add items to your cart and proceed to the "Checkout Page."
    • Enter your voucher code in the "Voucher" field under the Checkout Button
    • Apply the voucher by clicking "Redeem Voucher."

Step 4: Confirm Your Payment

Once your payment is complete, you'll receive an order confirmation email. If you've chosen online payment, the payment confirmation page will also display your payment details.

If you experience any issues with payment, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team via 0711 011 011. We're here to help and make sure that your shopping experience is as smooth as possible.
